How To Prepare Chapatis

This is some good food

Ingredients

  • 1kg Wheat Flour
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1.5 tablespoon sugar
  • 2 cups Warm water
  • Cooking oil

Procedure

  1. Mix all the elements(apar from the cooking oil) to a fine Dough
  2. Cut the dough into small handfull pieces and roll them into a ball
  3. Flatten the dough balls into a circular shape
  4. Add a tablespoon full of cooking oil to a heated frying pan
  5. Place the flattened dough on the pan and let it cook till golden brown
  6. Flip and cook the other side while adding some oil
  7. Repeat for the rest
  8. Serve with hot stew
