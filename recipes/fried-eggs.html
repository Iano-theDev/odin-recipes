Delicious Fried eggs

Nothing slaps in the morning like some fried eggs, a toast and some coffee. This is one of the most delicious and enticing delicacies on could easily enjoy. It is very easy to make fried eggs, making it a time saver if you have a busy morning. So, grab your eggs and frying pan and let's get frying...

Ingredients

  • two eggs
  • cooking oil
  • a pinch of salt
  1. Heat the pan
  2. Spread one spoon of oil on the hot pan
  3. Crack the eggs over the pan
  4. let them fry for 1 minute
  5. Flip them over if you like both sides fried
  6. let the eggs fry for another 30 seconds
  7. place the eggs on a clean plate
